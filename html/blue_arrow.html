Blue Arrow Baggage

Send your extra baggage to your destination and travel hassle free

By Dark Square | Analytics

Blue Arrow Baggage is one of the leading companies in arranging the most complex air, ocean or ground shipments. They take care of all aspects of the Supply Chain Management Process, which includes pick up from your shippers’ factory, custom clearance at origin , transportation, custom clearance at destination, warehousing, distribution and door delivery.


We recently developed a fully interactive, mobile optimized and reactive site for Blue Arrow Baggage! Including all the latest and greatest SEO and web development techniques to completely optimize the site for an amazing user experience.


Outside of all the SEO and web development techniques we implemented into this fully responsive site, we kicked it up a notch further! We did so by adding newly discovered features for embedded calls for users on a mobile device. This allows for web visitors on a mobile device to seamlessly get in contact with Blue Arrow Baggage without hesitation!


We also designed the ‘Book Now’ page to be extremely user friendly, and it also acts as an effective lead generation page for Blue Arrow Baggage. Generating tons of quality leads in real-time all day and night.


Lastly, to make sure Blue Arrow Baggage is keeping up with its customer acquisition we made sure to fully integrate data analytics software into the site to fully track web traffic. Needless to say, once we launched the new Blue Arrow Baggage site live, we saw an instant and permanent increase in traffic to the website.
